staring into the abyss of life

standing between the opposites

embracing it

grappling with it

asking it, understanding — is it worth it?

knowing it may not be

you ***can ***ask that

you may just go on because it is woven into the fabric of things to go on

you may simply choose to keep going, because things tend to keep going

it’s ok to not know why

standing on the precipice

pick one

dive into the flow of life once again

know you have choice
